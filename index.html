Donate Hardware

We collect and manage donated computing hardware for 24/7 scientific simulation. CPU-only systems are welcome, while modern GPUs deliver the highest impact. We currently serve the Chicago, Milwaukee, and Northern Indiana areas.

  • Modern NVIDIA GPUs (RTX 3060 or newer preferred)
  • CPU-only systems are accepted and put to productive use
  • Stable desktops with adequate cooling and power delivery
Email info@compute4cure.org

Note: We are not yet a 501(c)(3); donations are not currently tax-deductible.
